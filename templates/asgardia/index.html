Asgardia Foundation is one of the top NGOs in India for social, economic, skilling, livelihood, environmental protection, woman empowerment, employment schemes, education innovation, and health care awareness. Founded in Tamil Nadu, Chennai, in 2017. Our Foundation is well versed in Skilling for the Young Nation of India. Our Foundation programs focused on Vocation, Life, Soft, Technical Empowerment Skill, and Education Enhancement.
At Asgardia Foundation, our mission is dedicated to creating positive change in the lives of individuals and communities across India. We are dedicated to hrough our commitment to Mission, we strive to address pressing social issues and uplift those in need.

Our mission is to alleviate poverty and promote sustainable development by empowering communities through education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. We envision a world where every individual has access to basic necessities, enjoys equal opportunities, and lives in a sustainable environment, fostering a global community built on compassion and social justice
We envision a world where every individual has the opportunity to thrive through education, skills development, and empowerment. Our goal is to create a future where Education Scholarships enable all deserving students to pursue their dreams and unlock their full potential, regardless of their financial background. Employment Training equips individuals with the skills and knowledge necessary to secure meaningful and sustainable careers, fostering economic independence and personal growth.
  • Equality: Commitment to equal rights and opportunities for all.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct.
  • Empowerment: Empowering individuals and communities to take control of their own destinies.
  • Sustainability: Promoting practices that ensure long-term environmental and social sustainability.
  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships and collaborations to maximize impact.

Daily meals project and healthy food

The Asgardia Foundation's commitment to providing healthy food options is a commendable effort towards promoting well-being and nutrition.

Women Empowerment

Our Asgardia Foundation women through innovative community practices, empowering them to seek healthcare and bring sustainable changes in the community, while also supporting them to become aware, skilled, and financially independent.

Asgardia Unemployement Eradication scheme

Our scheme has enhanced the eradication of unemployment in the nation. Our track record has been proven by more than 1+ lakhs of employment in the nation.

Education

We believe that education is the key to unlocking the potential within every child, regardless of their economic background. We offer a supportive and nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, develop essential life skills, and gain the confidence needed to pursue their dreams.

Environmental protection

Plant native trees to sequester carbon and enhance ecosystem resilience. Develop and deliver educational programs to communities about climate change and adaptive strategies Integrate hygiene education into school curricula, emphasizing proper handwashing practices.

Health Care

Our Foundation's comprehensive and community-centric health programme takes primary healthcare services to the doorsteps of underserved communities in both rural and urban India
