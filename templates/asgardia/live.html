Livelihood

India's youth make up over a third of the nation's population, forming a significant portion of its labor force. With such a large young demographic, the country has the potential to capitalize on its demographic dividendâ€”provided that these young individuals are equipped with the right skills and employability. For sustainable economic growth and effective nation-building, it is essential to harness the potential of underprivileged youth by guiding their energy and talent in the right direction.

The Asgardia Foundation is committed to building sustainable livelihood opportunities for communities in need while ensuring their overall well-being through accessible healthcare programs. By focusing on skills development, employment support, and community health, the foundation aims to help individuals and families achieve financial stability, self-reliance, and a healthier lifestyle.

Key Livelihood Programs:

Skills Development & Training:
Conducts vocational training programs tailored to the needs of the community, including workshops on tailoring, handicrafts, and digital skills.
Offers soft skills training such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving, enhancing employability.
Job Placement Assistance:
Collaborates with local businesses and organizations to connect individuals with suitable job opportunities.
Provides career counseling and resume-building workshops to prepare individuals for job interviews and placements.
Community Health Camps:
Organizes health camps focusing on preventive care for children, women, and the elderly.
Provides free medical check-ups, consultations, and medications for those who cannot afford healthcare.
Maternal & Child Health:
Offers health support for pregnant women and new mothers, including prenatal and postnatal care.
Conducts awareness programs on nutrition and child healthcare.
Health Awareness & Education:
Spreads awareness about hygiene, disease prevention, and general well-being through community workshops and campaigns.
Distributes educational materials on health-related topics, ensuring that the community has access to vital information.
